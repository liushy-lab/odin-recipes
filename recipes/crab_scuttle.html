Crab Meat and Scuttle

Description

Mudcrab and scuttle are a beautiful combination of flavours that feature in this Dunmeri dip thatâ€™s a classic favourite.

Serve hot with saltrice crackers or scathecraw slices as a dip, or pour it over some ash yam chips with some fresh chilies to garnish for a spicy nacho treat!

Ingridients

  • 2 handfuls of scuttle
  • 4 pinches of wickwheat
  • 1 large kwama egg
  • the meat of one mudcrab (two portions)
  • 1 handful of chopped bittergreen

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, wickwheat, and scuttle in a large bowl
  2. Slowly stir in crabmeat and bittergreen
  3. Bake covered in a hot over [sic] for one half hour to one hour (when a knife comes out clean)
